Why choose GTC?

At GTC we welcome anyone with an interest in triathlon or any of the three disciplines. Whether you are a beginner or an Ironman we are sure that you will love being a member of our club. We offer two free training sessions for you to come and give us a try. This will give you the chance to meet the Club members, experience our coaching and get a feel for triathlon if you are new to the sport.
